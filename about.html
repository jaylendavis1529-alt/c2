The Crew Behind the Shine

QuickShine Auto started with one van, a pressure washer and a simple idea: detailing should come to you — and it should be done properly.

Our Story

Seven Years, 900+ Cars, One Standard

QuickShine Auto was founded in 2019 by two friends who kept hearing the same complaint: "I love a clean car, I just never have time to sit at a detail shop." So we flipped the model. We built out a fully self-contained mobile unit — water, power, professional products — and started bringing the shop to driveways, office lots and parking garages across New York City.

Seven years later the vans have multiplied, but the rule hasn't changed: every car gets treated like it's ours. That means no rushed jobs, no harsh brushes on your paint, no "good enough." Our technicians train for months before they touch a customer's vehicle, and every detail ends with a walk-around so you can inspect the work with us.

We're fully insured, we use eco-friendly and pH-balanced products, and we back everything with a simple promise — if you're not happy, we come back and make it right at no charge.
