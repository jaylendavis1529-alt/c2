Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones quickshineauto.com uses, and how you can control them. We designed our site so that optional cookies stay off until you say otherwise.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how they're used, and measure advertising. Similar technologies (like localStorage) are covered by this policy as well.

Cookies We Use

Strictly necessary (always on)

  • Consent preference: we store your cookie choice in your browser's localStorage (key: qsa_cookie_consent) so we don't ask you on every page. This contains only your choice — nothing personal.

Analytics (only with your consent)

  • Google Analytics 4 (cookies beginning with _ga): helps us understand which pages are useful, how visitors find us and where the site can improve. Data is aggregated; IP addresses are not logged or stored by GA4.

Advertising (only with your consent)

  • Google Ads conversion measurement: if you arrive from one of our ads, these cookies help us know our advertising works — including measuring calls made from ad phone numbers.

Google Consent Mode

We use Google Consent Mode v2. Until you make a choice in our banner, all analytics and advertising storage is set to "denied" — Google's tags receive no cookie data. If you choose "Accept all", consent is updated and the cookies above activate. If you choose "Necessary only", they remain off and the site works fully.

Managing Your Choice

  • To change your decision here, clear this site's data in your browser settings and the banner will reappear on your next visit.
  • You can also block or delete cookies globally in your browser's privacy settings.
  • To opt out of Google Analytics across all websites, Google provides a browser add-on.

Changes to This Policy

If our cookie usage changes, we will update this page and, where required, ask for your consent again.
