Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

QuickShine Auto ("we", "us", "our") operates quickshineauto.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or contact us, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep this simple and honest — the same way we run our business.

Information We Collect

  • Information you give us: when you call us, fill out our callback form, or email us, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, phone number, email address, vehicle information and service location. We use this solely to provide quotes and perform the services you request.
  • Information collected automatically: with your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site (pages viewed, approximate location, device type). This data is aggregated and does not directly identify you.
  • Call data: if you reach us through a phone number shown in an online advertisement, the advertising platform (such as Google Ads) may record that a call took place, its duration and the caller's number for measurement purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries, provide quotes and schedule and perform detailing services.
  • To improve our website, services and marketing with aggregated analytics.
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising, including call-based advertising.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties except service providers who help us operate (analytics, advertising measurement, scheduling), and only to the extent needed.

Cookies and Consent

We use cookies as described in our Cookie Policy. Analytics and advertising cookies are not set until you choose "Accept all" in our cookie banner. You can choose "Necessary only" at any time and the site will work fully.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live (including under GDPR and CCPA/CPRA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete or export the personal information we hold about you, and to opt out of certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below — we respond to all verified requests within 30 days.

Data Retention & Security

We keep customer records only as long as needed to provide services and meet legal obligations, then delete them. We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our services and website are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will post the updated version here with a new "Last updated" date.
