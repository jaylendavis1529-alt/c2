Detailing Packages for Every Ride

Every package below is performed at your location by insured, trained technicians. Prices vary with vehicle size and condition — call for an exact quote in under two minutes.

Exterior Detailing

Our signature exterior package starts with a pre-rinse and snow-foam bath to lift grit safely off the paint, followed by a two-bucket hand wash — never brushes, never swirl-inducing shortcuts. We clay-bar the paint to pull out embedded contaminants you can feel but can't see, deep clean the wheels, wheel wells and tires, dress the trim, and finish with a polymer sealant that keeps water beading for up to six months.

  • Snow foam + two-bucket hand wash
  • Clay bar decontamination
  • Wheels, tires & wheel wells detailed
  • 6-month polymer paint sealant
  • Streak-free glass, inside & out

Interior Deep Clean

Life happens inside your car — coffee runs, kids, pets, commutes. Our interior deep clean resets all of it. We vacuum and steam every surface, extract stains from carpets and upholstery, condition leather so it stays supple, and detail the spots most shops skip: air vents, seat rails, seals and cup holders. The result smells clean because it is clean — no perfume cover-ups.

  • Full vacuum + hot steam sanitation
  • Carpet & upholstery stain extraction
  • Leather cleaned and conditioned
  • Pet hair & odor removal available
  • Vents, seams & touchpoints hand-detailed

Ceramic Coating

A professional ceramic coating is the closest thing to armor for your paint. After full decontamination and a machine polish to perfect the surface, we apply a 9H professional-grade coating that chemically bonds with your clear coat. Expect years of intense gloss, effortless washes, and serious resistance to UV fading, bird droppings and road salts — with a written warranty.

  • Full decontamination + single-stage polish prep
  • 9H professional-grade ceramic coating
  • 2–5 year protection options
  • Hydrophobic: dirt & water slide off
  • Written warranty included

Paint Correction

Swirl marks, light scratches, water spots, holograms from bad buffing — paint correction removes them rather than hiding them. Using measured, multi-stage machine polishing, we level the clear coat until it reflects like glass. Every correction starts with a paint-depth reading so we work safely, and ends with a protective sealant to lock in the finish.

  • Paint depth measured before any polishing
  • 1–3 stage machine correction
  • Removes swirls, oxidation & light scratches
  • Finished with sealant or optional ceramic
  • Before/after photos of every panel
from $349
